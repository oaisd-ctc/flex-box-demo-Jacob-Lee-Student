Flex-Box Container Properties

Resize the page to see how the flex-wrap property effects the content

flex-wrap: nowrap; (default)

flex-wrap: wrap;

flex-flow

The flex-flow property is a combination of the flex-direction and the flex-flow properties. You can combine both in order to save space in your CSS.

flex-flow: row wrap;
